About Olivwin Transporters

At Olivwin Transporters, we’re redefining transport in Uganda — one ride at a time. From town hops to long-distance journeys, our clean, GPS-tracked vehicles and courteous drivers deliver reliability, comfort, and safety.

What began as a local idea in Kasese has grown into a community-driven movement committed to improving everyday mobility. Whether you're heading to the market, school, or airport — we’re just a tap away.

  • ✓ Locally owned and operated
  • ✓ Trusted by hundreds daily
  • ✓ Expanding across Western Uganda and beyond
